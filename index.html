Why are dogs so important?

Having a dog can make a persons life improve in dramatic ways. Some people have dogs as companions and others have dogs that provide them with a life changing service. Let us take a look at a few of the roles a dog can play in a persons life.


Service Dogs

A service dog is one that we see quite frequently. They provide different services to their owners in order to make their day to day life more manageable. A service dog can be the difference between life and death for those who have health issues.

Family Dogs

Most households have a dog that is a member of their family, and they trust them implicitly. Their children are raised around them and they offer their family a companionship that cannot be duplicated. For some people a dog is all that they have. This animal offers them companionship that they cannot get elsewhere.

In My Experience

My dog Sandy has offered me more than just companionship. She is a motive for me to work hard and better myself. The harder I work the more that I achieve and the better my life will be. Which in turn will improve her life. She offers me a constant comfort with her presence alone
